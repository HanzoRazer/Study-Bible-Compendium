## Original Language Notes

### צוּר (tsur)
- Strong's H6697
- Meaning: rock, cliff, rocky wall
- Usage: Often metaphorical for God as refuge/strength

### סֶלַע (sela)
- Strong's H5553
- Meaning: crag, cliff, rock
- Usage: Often a physical rock formation; also metaphorical
