## Original Language Notes

### מַטֶּה (matteh)
- Strong's H4294
- Used for both Moses' rod and Aaron's staff
- Context determines which instrument is in view

### Key passages
- Exodus 4:2-4 — Moses' rod (shepherd's staff, becomes serpent)
- Exodus 7:9-12 — Aaron's staff (cast before Pharaoh)
- Numbers 17:8 — Aaron's staff (budded, blossomed, produced almonds)
